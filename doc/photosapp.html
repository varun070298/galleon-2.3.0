Photos

Title: The title of the app.
Use safe viewing area: Check to specify that photos should fit within the safe viewing area of the TV. Use this options if your photos appear go outside of the visible screen.
Random play folders: Enable random play of photos when playing folders.
Slideshow Effects: The list of available transitions between photos during a slideshow.
Display Time: The time in seconds the photo should be displayed during a slideshow.
Transition Time: The time in seconds for the transition effect between photos in a slideshow.
Name: The name of the directory of image files.
Path: The path of the directory of image files. JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP. WBMP formats are supported.

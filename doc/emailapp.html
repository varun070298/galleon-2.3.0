Email

Title: The title of the app.
Reload: The period in minutes for checking the mail servers.
Download Limit: Limit the number of emails that are downloaded per account.
Name: The name of the mail server.
Protocol: The protocol of the mail server. Use either POP3S, or IMAPS for secured connections.
Server: The internet address of the mail server.
Username: The username for the mail account.
Password: The password for the mail account.
